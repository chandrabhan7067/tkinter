India vs England, 5th Test, Day 2 Highlights: Riding on Jasprit Bumrah's 3/35 and a wicket each from Mohammed Shami and Mohammed Siraj, India reduced England to 84/5 on Day 2 of the ongoing fifth Test at Edgbaston, Birmingham. Bumrah led from the front as he dismissed England openers Alex Lees and Zak Crawley before claiming the wicket of Ollie Pope to put India in complete control against the hosts.
